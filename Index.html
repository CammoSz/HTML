Course Overview

Our first E-Sports course is designed to prepare our students for careers in E-Sports by providing them with the equipment to play competitive games such as CSGO and League of Legends along with building up their knowledge about E-sports and the competitive scene. This will provide experince in competitive gaming which will assist our students in procuring a career in E-Sports gaming.

What will I learn?

As part of the course you will be given an introduction to E-sports, providing context to the other units. You will learn about entrepreneurship in the E-sports industry and market research upon that subject. You will also learn about issues in E-sports with player wellbeing and health being an important subject.

What equipment do I need?

The equipment used will be provided on site as part of the course, however you are allowed to bring your own devices like laptops. It is very important that you have a computer at home with internet access.

Where will I study?

You'll be studying in a lab outfitted with mid-range computer systems fit for gaming.

How will I be assessed?

There are 4 units to be completed in the course. Each unit has an assignment that is to be completed by computer.
